Supermarket shopping pattern analysis

This project analyzes customer shopping patterns in a supermarket using the Apriori algorithm. It discovers which products are frequently purchased together and provides insights for better store layout and promotions.

Tools Used
Python
Pandas
mlxtend

 Code Parts
The code has 3 main parts:

Part A: Data Preparation
Shows customer shopping lists
Converts data to computer-friendly format(one hot encoded format)

Part B: Apriori Algorithm
Finds frequent itemsets in the dataset
Creates shopping rules
Shows support, confidence, lift numbers

Part C: Interpretation
Shows top 3 strongest rules and explains what they mean
Gives business recomendations

Business recommendations

1The supermarket should place beer shelves close to the diaper section to increase convienience .
   
They can offer a conditional discount where customers who purchase more than two items such as milk, bread and eggs receive a small price reduction on eggs or any other item, encouraging complementary purchases 

What the Numbers Mean
Support: This is the probability of items appearing together, indicating item's frequency and common co-purchase 
Confidence: It reflects the likehood of item y being purchased given item x, representing the reliability
Lift: How strong the connection is between item x and item y
